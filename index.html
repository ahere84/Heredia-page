About

I’m an undergraduate CS student at UMGC focused on microcontroller firmware and companion apps that interface with hardware. I build end-to-end prototypes edge computing to explore new interaction platforms like eye-tracking nurse-call systems and wearable Privacy-Preserving AI devices. I’ve been building and troubleshooting hardware since I was young, and I enjoy tackling problems across electronics, firmware, software, and UX.
